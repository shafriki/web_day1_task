My Travel Blog


Our rag tour was an unforgettable experience. Our destination was Cox's Bazar and Saint Martin's Island, and almost everyone from our batch joined the trip. We had an amazing time exploring these beautiful locations together. At Cox's Bazar, we visited Sugandha Beach, where the golden sands and rhythmic waves created a mesmerizing atmosphere. Walking along the shore with friends, we shared laughter and stories, capturing countless memories in photos. The fun didn’t stop there; we also explored the surrounding areas, each spot adding to our joy and excitement. Saint Martin’s Island was another highlight of the trip. The crystal-clear waters and serene environment provided a perfect escape from our routine. We indulged in local delicacies, played beach games, and enjoyed the stunning sunsets. This rag tour strengthened our bonds and gave us memories to cherish forever.
